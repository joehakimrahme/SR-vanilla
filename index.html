Rules

  • You get assigned a race, 3 Major Skills, 3 Minor Skills, a Quest, zero or more handicaps
  • The game ends when you finish the assigned quest's mainline. You're free to do whatever before then.
  • Until you reach level 20, you can only spend perk points on your Major Skill trees.
  • After you reach 20, you can also spend perk points on Minor Skill trees.
  • Once you've allocated the lvl100 perk of all of your skills, you may spend future perk points on anything you want
